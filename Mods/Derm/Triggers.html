This Page Will Explain The Trigger Block Version 2.0.0 Or Higher


Theese Blocks Are Redstone Enabled Blocks, With A Matching Trigger Card

There Are 2 Types Of Theese Blocks, Pulse And Toggle

The Pulse Type, When Activated With It's Card It Will Send A Redstone Pulse For About 30 Ticks

The Toggle Type, When Activated With It's Card It Will Toggle It's Redstone State From On To Off And Vice Versa

The Cards Are Colour Coded To Match The Trigger Blocks Regardless Of Type
